What is YBI

Young Black and Intelligent derived from brainstorming to define what we are, and who we plan to reach and serve throughout life. Young Black & Intelligent is a multifaceted business that focuses on Education, Holistic Health, Entrepreneurship, and Black Economics! Through literature, academic sessions, and video content we hope to reach the masses of our people to influence them to become the highest version of themselves! Please support our page and our mission to uplift our people and influence generations to come!
